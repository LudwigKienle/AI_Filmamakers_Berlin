AI Filmmaker Meetup

24 January 2025, workish.berlin

For our January 2025 meetup, we brought together filmmakers and AI enthusiasts at workish.berlin in Neukölln to explore the latest trends in AI-assisted filmmaking. It was a fantastic evening connecting seasoned professionals and newcomers to network, share ideas, and spark new collaborations.

Highlight of the evening: We hosted a special screening of cutting-edge AI-powered short films that beautifully showcased the incredible creative potential of this rapidly evolving technology.

Our community-led events consistently foster deeper connections amongst artists and set the stage for groundbreaking creative partnerships. If your organization is interested in future collaborations or hosting one of our upcoming sessions, please reach out. We are always looking to partner with forward-thinking spaces and tech platforms to push the boundaries of AI cinema together!
