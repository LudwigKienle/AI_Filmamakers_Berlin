AI Filmmaker Meetup

4 April 2025, Saturday Monster Challenge Special

Our April 2025 meetup at workish.berlin brought the local AI filmmaking community together for an unforgettable night of creative exchange. We gathered seasoned filmmakers and curious innovators to discuss the cutting-edge trends shaping the future of storytelling.

Highlights of the evening

  • An exclusive screening session featuring some of the latest AI-powered short films, demonstrating what modern tools are truly capable of.
  • Live Prompting with the SMC - Saturday Monster Challenge: We hosted an explosive live prompting session in collaboration with SMC, where attendees got hands-on experience crafting AI visuals in real-time alongside industry peers.

These dynamic, community-driven meetups are designed to foster deep connections and launch bold new creative collaborations. We are actively seeking visionary partners who want to support the next era of AI filmmaking. If you represent an organization or tech platform looking to sponsor or co-host future groundbreaking events, we'd love to hear from you!
